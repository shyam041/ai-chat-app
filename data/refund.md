# Refund & Return Policy

_Last updated: January 1, 2026_

Welcome to **NextEcommerce**. We strive to ensure that you are completely satisfied with your purchase. If you are not satisfied, we're here to help.

---

## 📦 Returns

You have **7 days** from the date of delivery to request a return.

To be eligible for a return:
- The item must be unused and in the same condition that you received it.
- The item must be in the original packaging.
- You must provide a valid receipt or proof of purchase.

### Non-returnable items:
- Gift cards
- Downloadable software products
- Personal care items (if opened)
- Perishable goods

---

## 🔁 Refunds

Once we receive your returned item, we will inspect it and notify you of the status.

If approved:
- Refund will be processed within **5–7 business days**
- Amount will be credited to your original payment method

If rejected:
- We will notify you with the reason for rejection

---

## 🚚 Shipping

- Customers are responsible for return shipping costs unless the item is defective or incorrect
- Shipping costs are non-refundable
- We recommend using a trackable shipping service

---

## ❌ Cancellations

Orders can be cancelled:
- Before they are shipped
- Within **2 hours** of placing the order

Once shipped, cancellation is not possible, but you may request a return.

---

## ⚠️ Damaged or Incorrect Items

If you receive a damaged or wrong item:
1. Contact us within **48 hours** of delivery
2. Share images of the product
3. We will arrange a replacement or full refund

---

## 💳 Late or Missing Refunds

If you haven’t received a refund yet:
- Check your bank account again
- Contact your credit card company (processing delays may occur)
- Contact your bank

If you’ve done all of this and still have not received your refund, please contact us.
